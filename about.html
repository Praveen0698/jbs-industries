Our Mission and Vission

Since inception, ensuring customer satisfaction has been our one & only mission. Every time a customer places an order with us, our team’s first responsibility is to understand the customer’s requirements. Our manufacturing experts strongly analyze the specifications provided by customer, and accordingly tailor stylish, aesthetically pleasing, and fully functional uPVC doors and windows at affordable prices.

With all these years of market experience and portfolio, JBS Industries Windoor has earned a trust-worthy reputation of being the #1 upvc doors & windows manufacturer in Odisha. But our vision is not to limit our brand reputation up to here, we want to serve the best-quality and eco-friendly upvc doors and upvc windows in Bhubaneswar for the sake of sustainability.
